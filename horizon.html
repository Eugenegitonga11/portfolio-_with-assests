About Horizon Bank

Horizon Bank is a premier national bank that aspires to power developments and financial freedom in South Sudan. At Horizon Bank, it is about people first, and our vision for the future is to provide our clients with the financial services that look to grow and empower them.

Our Vision

To be the trusted national premier Bank and preferred financial services provider with world-class solutions.

Our Mission

To deliver world-class products and services to our customers.
To attract, develop, and retain the best talent in South Sudan.
To support the communities in which we operate in by becoming a good Corporate Citizen.

Core Values

  • Integrity: We are committed to conducting ourselves in a manner consistent with our business.
  • Prudence: We exercise good judgment and common sense in all we do.
  • Excellence: We strive to redefine the standard of excellence in everything we do.
  • Collaboration: We recognize we won’t thrive without recognizing one another’s strengths.
